About

Through clinical practice, research, and strategic analysis, I have seen that breakthroughs alone are not enough. Real impact depends on how effectively science connects with healthcare systems, industry, policy, and investment communities.

I am particularly interested in roles and collaborations at the intersection of healthcare innovation, cross-sector ecosystem building, venture and investment, philanthropy, and translational strategy.

My focus is on building bridges across academia, healthcare, industry, government, and venture so that promising ideas can move faster toward real-world implementation.
